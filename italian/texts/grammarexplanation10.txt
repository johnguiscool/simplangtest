<b>Voglio ...</b> means [I want ... ].  As in English, it can be used to express wanting a thing </i>or</i> wanting to do something.

<p>Wanting a thing:</p>

<ul>
<li><b>Voglio una macchina.</b><br>I want a car.</li>
<li><b>Voglio studiare un libro.</b><br>I want a book.</li>
<li><b>Voglio una TV.</b><br>I want a TV.</li>
</ul>

<p>Wanting to do something:</p>

<ul>
<li><b>Voglio studiare in Italia.</b><br>I want to study in Italy.</li>
<li><b>Voglio viviere in Italia.</b><br>I want to live in Italy.</li>
<li><b>Voglio pranzare con ti.</b><br>I want to have lunch with you.</li>
</ul>
