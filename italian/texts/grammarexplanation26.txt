As you have seen in previous lessons, verbs in the future tense end with -ò in the first person.  You can see that again in the following sentences:

<ul>
<li><b>Stasera leggerò un libro a casa.</b> <br> Today evening, I will be reading a book at home.</li>
<li><b>Io lavorerò su un progetto.</b> <br> I will work on a project.</li>
</ul>

Verbs in the future tense end with -ai in the second person (informal).  

<ul>
<li><b>Tu farai.</b> <br> You will do.</li>
<li><b>Tu lavorerai.</b> <br> You will work.</li>
<li><b>Tu leggerai.</b> <br> You will read.</li>
</ul>
