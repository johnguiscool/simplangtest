In this dialogue, you can see how future tense is formed for the second person:

<ul>
<li><b>Andrai.</b><br> You will go.  </li>
<li><b>Partirai.</b><br> You will leave. </li>
</ul>

<p>
The future tense for the first person for these two verbs is as follows:

<li><b>Andrò.</b><br> You will go.  </li>
<li><b>Partirò.</b><br> You will leave. </li>

</p>
