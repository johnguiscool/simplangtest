<b>È difficile ... ?</b> means [Is it difficult ... ?].  Note that in this phrase, the pronoun [it] is omitted.  

<p><b>Lui</b> is the pronoun meaning [He] or [Him].</p>

<p><b>Lei</b> is the pronoun meaning [She] or [Her].</p>

<p><b>Si</b> is a pronoun that has many uses in Italian.  In most contexts, it means [Itself / Himself / Herself].  <b>Che si trova in ... </b> means [That finds itself in ...].
