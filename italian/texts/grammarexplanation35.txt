<b>Di fretta</b> is the Italian phrase for [in a hurry].  

<p> In this dialogue, we can see more uses of verbs in the first-person plural conjugation ending in <b>-iamo</b>. </p>

Expressing the indicative mood:

<ul>
<li><b>Possiamo incontraci.</b><br>We can meet.</li>
<li><b>John e io ci sposiamo.</b><br>John and I are getting married.</li>
</ul>

Expressing the imperative mood:

<ul>
<li><b>Prendiamo un caffè.</b><br>Let's get a coffee.</li>
</ul>
