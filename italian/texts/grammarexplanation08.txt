<b>Ho ...</b> means [I have ...].  Here are some examples of how the verb can be used:

<ul>
<li><b>Ho una domanda.</b><br> I have a question.</li>
<li><b>Ho una macchina.</b><br> I have a car.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Si dice</b> is a slightly idiomatic expression that means [One says].  <b>Come si dice ... ?</b> thus translates to [How does one say ... ?]</p>

<p><b>Dimmi</b> means [Tell me] in the imperative mood.</p>
