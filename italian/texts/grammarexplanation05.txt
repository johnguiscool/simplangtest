<b>Devo...</b> is a modal verb meaning I should.  The following examples show how the verb can be used:

<ul>
<li><b>Devo andare al lavoro.</b><br> I should go to work. </li>
<li><b>Devo viaggiare.</b><br> I should travel. </li>
<li><b>Devo guardare la TV.</b><br> I should watch TV.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Ti va di incontrarci domani?</b> is an idiomatic way to ask if someone wants to meet.  <p><b>Ti va di ... ?</b> can be used to express an invitation to any activity.</p>
