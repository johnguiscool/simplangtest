<h4>Forming The Present Continuous Tense 2/2</h4>

To form yes/no questions in the present continuous tense, add the <b>-iyor</b> suffix and the question particle according to the table below:
<p>
<b>yapmak</b> - to do
<p>

<table>
<tr>
<td><b>(ben) yapiyor miyim? </b></td>
    <td>Do I do?</td>
    <td><b>(biz) yapiyor musunuz?</b></td>
    <td>Do we do?</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td><b>(sen) yapiyor musun?</b> </td>
<td>Do you do?</td>
<td><b>(siz) yapiyor muyuz?</b></td>
<td>Do you do? (formal)</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td><b>(o) yapiyor mi?</b> </td>
<td>Does he do?</td>
<td><b>(onlar) yapiyorlar mi?</b></td>
<td>Do they do?</td>
</tr>

</table>
