There are two ways to make an invitation.  <b>Ti va di + [activity] </b> (literally, [Does it go for you to (do this activity)?] can be used to invite someone to an activity.  <b>Ti andrebbe di + [activity] </b> uses the conditional form (literally, [Would it go for you to (do this activity)?] and expresses more politeness and less expectation.

<ul>
<li><b>Ti va di andare a cena insieme stasera?</b><br> Do you want to get dinner with me this evening?</li>
<li><b>Ti andrebbe di andare a cena insieme stasera?</b><br> Would you like to get dinner with me this evening?</li>
</ul>
