<b>Voglio andare in Inghiterra</b> means [I want to go to England].

<p>
<b>Vorrei andare in Inghilterra.</b> means [I would like to go to England].

<p>
<b>Vorrei ...</b> can be used to express [I would like ...] in a number of different situations.

<ul>
<li><b>Vorrei vivere in Italia.</b> I would like to live in Italy. </li>
<li><b>Vorrei un caffe.</b> I would like a coffee. </li>
</ul>
