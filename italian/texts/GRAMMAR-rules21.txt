<h4></h4>

Because the verb "to be" does not exist in Turkish, to express a sentence like "I will be a teacher", Turkish speakers use the verb <b>olmak</b>, meaning "to become".  In Turkish, there is no grammatical distinction between "I will be ___" and "I will become ___".

<p>
<b>Ben öğretmen olacağım.</b> - I will be a teacher. / I will become a teacher.

<p>
<b>yapmak</b> - to do
