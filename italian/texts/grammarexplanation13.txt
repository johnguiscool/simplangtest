We have already seen that the verb <b>piace</b> can be used to describe liking to do an activity.

<p>When expressing liking things, we also use the verb <b>piace</b>.  <b>Piace</b> is used when talking about a singular noun.  <b>Piacciono</b> is used when talking about a plural noun.</p>

<p><b>Mi piace ...</b> is used to express [I like ... ].</p>

<p><b>Ti piace ...</b> and <b>A te piace ... </b> can both be used to express [You like ... ].</p>

<ul>
<li><b>Mi piace Roma.</b><br> I like Rome.</li>
<li><b>Ti piace il tuo lavoro?</b><br> Do you like your work?</li>

<li><b>Mi piacciono i turisti.</b><br> I like tourists.</li>
<li><b>Ti piacciono i tui fratelli?</b><br> Do you like your brothers?</li>

</ul>
