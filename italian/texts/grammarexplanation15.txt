<b>Io lo voglio</b> literally means [I want it], because <b>lo</b> is the pronoun for [it].  In this dialogue, the <b>lo</b> in the phrase <b>Io lo voglio</b> refers to <b>visitare questo paese</b> or [visiting this village].

<p>To express needing something, Italians use the phrase, <b>Ho bisogno di ...</b> which translates literally to [I have need of ... ].  This phrase can be used with verbs or nouns:</p>

<ul>
<li><b>Ho bisogno di una macchina.</b><br> I need a car.  </li>
<li><b>Ho bisogno di lavorare.</b><br> I need to work. </li>
</ul>
