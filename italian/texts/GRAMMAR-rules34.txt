<h4>Pronouns</h4>

The subject pronouns in Turkish are as follows:
<p>
<table>

<tr>
<td><b>ben</b></td>
<td>I</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td><b>sen</b></td>
<td>you</td>
</tr>


<tr>
<td><b>o</b></td>
<td>he / she</td>
</tr>


<tr>
<td><b>biz</b></td>
<td>we</td>
</tr>


<tr>
<td><b>siz</b></td>
<td>you (formal / plural)</td>
</tr>


<tr>
<td><b>onlar</b></td>
<td>they</td>
</tr>

</table>
