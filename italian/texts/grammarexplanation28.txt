In this dialogue, which takes place during a formal situation between two strangers (a job interview), Marcello and Sofia speak to each other in the formal you rather than the informal you.  Here is a comparison of typical phrases in the informal you and the formal you.

<ul>
<li><b>Come ti chiami?</b><br> What's your name? (informal)</li>
<li><b>Come si chiama?</b><br> What's your name? (formal)</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><b>Da dove vieni?</b><br> Where are you from? (informal)</li>
<li><b>Da dove viene?</b><br> Where are you from? (formal)</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><b>Perché vuoli lavorare in Italia?</b><br>Why do you want to work in Italy? (informal)</li>
<li><b>Perché vuole lavorare in Italia?</b><br>Why do you want to work in Italy? (formal)</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><b>È la tua prima volta in Italia?</b><br>Is it your first time in Italy?  (informal)</li>
<li><b>È la sua prima volta in Italia?</b><br>Is it your first time in Italy? (informal)</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><b>Dove è stata in Italia?</b><br>Where were you in Italy?  (informal)</li>
