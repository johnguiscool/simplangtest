The suffix <b>-mak</b> can also used to create the noun form of a verb.  In practice there are two ways to form the noun form of a verb:

<ul>
<li><b>buluşmak</b><br>to meet up</li>
<li><b>buluşmayı</b><br>to meet up</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><b>Çalışmak istiyorum.</b><br>I want to work.</li>
<li><b>Çalışmayı seviyorum.</b><br>I like to work.</li>
</ul>

The <b>-mak</b> suffix is used to form the infinitive when it is the subject of a sentence, and when used with the verb <b>istiyorum / istiyorsun</b> (I like to / you like to).

<p>

The <b>-mayı</b> suffix is used to form the infinitive when it is the object of a verb (other than <b>istiyorum / istiyorsun</b>).
