<h4>Imperatives</h4>

To form commands in Turkish, simply use the stem of the verb.

<p>

When giving commands to someone whom you would address using the formal "you" <b>siz</b>, add the suffix <b>-iniz, -ınız</b>.

<p>For commands in the first person plural ("Let's..."), add the suffix <b>-elim, -alım</b>.

<p>
<b>gitmek</b> - to go
