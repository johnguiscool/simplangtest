<h4>The Simple Past Tense 2/2</h4>

To form yes/no questions in the present continuous tense, add the <b>-ti / -di</b> suffix followed by the question particle according to the table below:
<p>
<b>yapmak</b> - to do
<p>

<table>
<tr>
<td><b>(ben) yaptım mı?</b></td>
    <td>Did I do?</td>
    <td><b>(biz) yaptık mı?</b></td>
    <td>Did we do?</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td><b>(sen) yaptın mı?</b> </td>
<td>Did you do?</td>
<td><b>(siz) yaptınız mı?</b></td>
<td>Did you do? (formal)</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td><b>(o) yaptı mı?</b> </td>
<td>Did he do?</td>
<td><b>(onlar) yaptılar mı?</b></td>
<td>Did they do?</td>
</tr>

</table>
