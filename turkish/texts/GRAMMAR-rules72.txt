
<b>Çünkü</b> is the Turkish word for "because".

<p>

<b>Şemsiyeni almalısın çünkü yağmur yağıyor.</b> - You should take your umbrella because it is raining.
<br>
<b>Bugün erken uyuyacağım çünkü başım ağırıyor.</b> - I will sleep early today because I have a headache.
<br>
<b>Sıkıldım çünkü film çok uzundu.</b> - I was bored because the film was too long.
<br>
<b>Sarah sinemaya gelemedi çünkü hastaydı.</b> - Sarah couldn't come to the cinema because she was ill.

<hr>
<p>

<b>Çünkü</b> can not be used as an introductory dependent clause at the beginning of a sentence.  Instead, clauses starting with <b>çünkü</b> should always come after the main clause of a sentence.
<p>
<font color ="red"> ✗ <b>Çünkü yağmur yağıyor. Şemsiyeni almalısın.</b> </font>- Because it is raining, you should take your umbrella.

<br>
<font color="green">✔  <b>Şemsiyeni almalısın. Çünkü yağmur yağıyor.</b> </font>- You should take your umbrella. Because it is raining.
