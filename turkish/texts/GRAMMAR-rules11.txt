<h4></h4>

Because the verb "to be" does not exist in Turkish, Turkish speakers have a special suffix for expressing that something "was" something in the past.

<p>

[Adjective] + <b>-di / -du</b>

<p>
<b>Sınav zordu.</b> - The exam was difficult.
<br>
<b>O çok güzeldi.</b> - She was very beautiful.

<p>

[Noun] + <b>-di / -du</b>

<p>
<b>Ben öğrenciydim.</b> - I was a student.
<b>O öğrenmendi.</b> - He was a teacher.
