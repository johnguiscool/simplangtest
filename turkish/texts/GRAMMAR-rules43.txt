<h4>Verbs of Motion</h4>

<b>Gitmek</b> is the verb meaning "to go".  It is conjugated as follows in the present tense:

<p>
<table>
<tr>
<td><b>(ben) gidiyorum </b></td>
    <td>I go</td>
    <td><b>(biz) gidiyoruz</b></td>
    <td>we go</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td><b>(sen) gidiyorsun </b> </td>
<td>you go</td>
<td><b>(siz) gidiyorsunuz</b></td>
<td>you go (formal)</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td><b>(o) gidiyor </b> </td>
<td>he goes</td>
<td><b>(onlar) gidiyorlar</b></td>
<td>they go</td>
</tr>

</table>
