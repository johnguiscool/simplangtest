<h4>Forming The Present Simple Tense 2/2</h4>

To form yes/no questions in the present simple tense, add the <b>-r</b> suffix and the question particle according to the table below:
<p>
<b>yapmak</b> - to do
<p>

<table>
<tr>
<td><b>(ben) yapar mıyım? </b></td>
    <td>Do I do?</td>
    <td><b>(biz) yapar mıyız?</b></td>
    <td>Do we do?</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td><b>(sen) yapar mısın?</b> </td>
<td>Do you do?</td>
<td><b>(siz) yapar mısınız?</b></td>
<td>Do you do? (formal)</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td><b>(o) yapar mı?</b> </td>
<td>Does he do?</td>
<td><b>(onlar) yaparlar mı?</b></td>
<td>Do they do?</td>
</tr>

</table>
