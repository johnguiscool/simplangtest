<b>Bom dia</b> is the greeting used in the morning.
﻿<p><b>﻿Boa tarde</b> is the greeting used in the afternoon.

<p><b>Estou...</b> is a verb meaning [I am ...], and it can be used to express one's current state <i>or</i> to express that one is currently doing some action.</p>

<p>Using <b>Estou...</b> to express one's current state:</p>

<ul>
<li><b>Estou bem.</b><br>I'm well.</li>
</ul>

<p>Using <b>Estou...</b> to express that one is currently doing some action:</p>

<ul>
<li><b>Estou a ler um livro.</b><br>I'm reading a book.</li>
<li><b>Estou a ir para o trabalho.</b><br>I'm going to work.</li>
</ul>
