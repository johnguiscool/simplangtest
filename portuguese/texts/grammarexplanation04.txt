<b>Gostas de ... ?</b> means [Do you like ... ?], and can be used to ask someone if they like to perform some action.

<p><b>Sim</b> is the Portuguese word for [Yes].</p>

<p><b>Não</b> is the Portuguese word for [No].</p>

<p>The word <b>não</b> can also be used to express negation:</p>

<ul>
<li><b>Não gosto de ver televisão..</b><br> I don't like watching TV.</li>
</ul>
